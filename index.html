About

Building drawings people can build from.

I'm a civil engineer with a strong foundation in AutoCAD drafting and Building Information Modeling (BIM). My interest lies in translating structural and architectural intent into clean, dimensionally accurate drawings — the kind that hold up on site, not just on screen.

I care about the details most people skip past: consistent layers, correct annotation, coordinated models, and drawings that make a contractor's job easier instead of harder. I'm currently looking to grow further into BIM coordination and detailed structural drafting.

Outside of drafting, I'm continually sharpening my modeling workflow — moving from 2D documentation toward fully coordinated 3D BIM models using Revit and Navisworks.
